Welcome to the inaugural issue of Ideas Worth Sharing.

In this essay, we break down why B2B founders are losing premium positioning.

## The Problem

Every developer, agency, and consultant starts by selling execution. They write the code, configure the servers, or build the spreadsheets. But as soon as execution is commoditized (by overseas labor or AI assistants), their margins compress.

The only way to preserve margins and grow is to build **authority**.

## What is Authority?

Authority is when a client trusts your *thinking* before they buy your *labor*. It is built by:
1. **Documenting your process**: Write down the exact steps you take to solve a problem. Give it a name.
2. **Publishing in public**: Share your case studies, logic breakdowns, and mistakes.
3. **Saying no to generic work**: Only take on projects that fit your unique positioning.

When you do this, you move from an execution resource to a strategic partner.

## Actionable Focus

This week, look at your primary landing page. Does it list your *features* or your *belief about the industry*? If it's features, rewrite it to explain your point-of-view.
