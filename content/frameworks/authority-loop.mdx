The Authority Loop is an editorial pipeline that turns standard business insights into inbound demand. It operates on a continuous four-stage cycle.

## The Cycle

1. **Synthesize**: Document real-world client breakthroughs and operational bottlenecks.
2. **Refine**: Apply clear typography, minimal design, and concise editing to present the insight.
3. **Syndicate**: Publish on professional platforms (like LinkedIn) and send to the newsletter list.
4. **Convert**: Direct readers to structured frameworks and free diagnostic resources.

## Real-World Application

We implement the Authority Loop by:
- Creating a 6-week content calendar based on core business problems.
- Using templates that prioritize typography and minimal design over flashy images.
- Adding high-value text links to deep-dive essays and scorecard forms.
