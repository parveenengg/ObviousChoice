The Positioning Matrix is a diagnostic tool that maps a B2B startup's capabilities against two axes: **Operational Velocity** and **Strategic Depth**.

## The Axes

1. **Strategic Depth (Vertical)**: Moves from low value (generic feature checklists) to high value (proprietary intellectual property and custom business models).
2. **Operational Velocity (Horizontal)**: Moves from slow (manual, labor-intensive agencies) to fast (AI-driven, automated systemized delivery).

The goal is to move your business to the top-right quadrant: high strategic depth paired with high operational velocity (AI-augmented consulting).

## Real-World Application

We use the Positioning Matrix to:
- Audit and plot competitor landing pages.
- Define a founder's unique perspective that sets them apart.
- Structure a service offering that combines high-level strategy with automated delivery.
