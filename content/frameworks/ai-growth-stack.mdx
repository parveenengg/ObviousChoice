The AI Growth Stack is a lightweight operational architecture that integrates automated LLM logic into B2B business workflows.

## The Architecture

1. **Automation Layer**: n8n, Python cron runners, or Node.js workers to trigger events.
2. **Context Layer**: Vector search and semantic databases (Pinecone, pgvector) containing business guidelines, templates, and frameworks.
3. **Execution Layer**: API calls to advanced models (Claude, Gemini, GPT-4o) with strict schema matching (structured output).
4. **Validation Gate**: A minimal admin UI where a human reviews and approves outputs before they are sent to clients.

## Real-World Application

We deploy the AI Growth Stack to automate:
- **Client Diagnostics**: Crawling a client's website, analyzing the messaging, and drafting a custom PDF scorecard.
- **Reporting Pipelines**: Aggregating weekly metrics and summarizing them into bulleted, professional summaries.
