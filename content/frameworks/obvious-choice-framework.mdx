The Obvious Choice Framework is a structured three-pillar audit designed to shift a B2B founder from a commodity provider to the default choice in their niche.

## Core Pillars

1. **Positioning Alignment**: Defining the exact buyer segment where your value is highest.
2. **Authority Asset Creation**: Writing down your intellectual property so client trust is built before the first sales call.
3. **AI Growth Systems**: Systemizing lead generation, onboarding, and reporting using lightweight custom software pipelines.

## Real-World Application

When consulting with B2B founders, we audit their current materials against these three pillars:
- **Phase 1**: Audit website messaging and LinkedIn positioning.
- **Phase 2**: Document their proprietary process into a visual diagram.
- **Phase 3**: Set up automated n8n workflows to collect client data and generate customized audit reports.
